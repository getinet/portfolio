The House

SUMMARY

Larry and Angela Tse opened this original location of The House in May of 1994 bringing their unique brand of Asian American food to San Francisco’s North Beach neighborhood. The House embodies the entrepreneurial spirit of growing up Asian in America with an emphasis on freshness and quality of ingredients, and a distinctively Asian philosophy towards food preparation.
