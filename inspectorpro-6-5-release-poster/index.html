InspectorPro Release Poster

Client: Beezwax

Role: Graphic Designer

For the launch of InspectorPro 6.5 at DevCon 17 (now Claris Engage), I designed this promotional poster to introduce the latest version of the application. InspectorPro, developed by Beezwax, is a powerful analysis tool for FileMaker solutions, providing deep insights into solution structure, performance, and security.

Design Approach

The goal of this poster was to visually communicate the core values of InspectorPro: collaboration, performance, and insight. The design features:

  • A bold, atmospheric composition that evokes exploration and discovery, aligning with the idea of gaining deeper insights into FileMaker solutions.
  • A custom gauge graphic placed at the center, reinforcing the theme of performance monitoring and system diagnostics.
  • Large, subtle typography integrating "DEVCON 17" into the background to tie the release to the event.
  • A clean and modern layout, keeping the focus on the product while maintaining visual interest.

Impact

The poster served as a key visual for InspectorPro’s presence at DevCon 17, supporting the product's positioning as an essential tool for FileMaker developers. The design contributed to brand awareness and engagement at the event, helping to showcase Beezwax’s commitment to innovation in the FileMaker ecosystem.

This project was an exciting opportunity to blend visual storytelling with technical precision, ensuring the design aligned with both the functionality and philosophy of InspectorPro.
