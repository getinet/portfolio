Rice & De Tienne Designs

When I designed the branding for Rice & De Tienne Designs, my goal was to create an identity as bold and refined as their work. Stephen Rice and Lynn De Tienne specialize in high-end residential architecture and interior design, crafting spaces that are both elegant and deeply personal.

Their design philosophy—balancing structure, detail, and livability—inspired every aspect of the visual identity.

The color palette is intentionally bold, making a strong, memorable mark that reflects the confidence and uniqueness of their designs. It stands out, just as their work does, ensuring their brand is as distinctive as the spaces they create. The oversized typography reinforces this presence, emphasizing their expertise and collaborative approach.

Every element of the branding was crafted to mirror Rice & De Tienne’s ability to blend creativity with precision. The result is an identity that feels modern, sophisticated, and unmistakably theirs.
