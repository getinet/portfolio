SF Ferry Terminal

Client: KMD Architects

As the graphic designer for the SF Ferry Terminal proposal cover, created on behalf of KMD Architects, my goal was to craft a visually engaging design that reflects both the architectural grandeur and civic significance of the project. KMD Architects, known for their innovative and sustainable design approach, is a firm dedicated to creating spaces that enhance the human experience and contribute to the environment. I wanted the design to align with their values, emphasizing modernity, clarity, and thoughtful design.

The cover integrates clean lines and dynamic imagery to convey the scale and innovative nature of the terminal. A carefully selected color palette balances professionalism with energy, ensuring the cover is both sophisticated and eye-catching. The imagery and typography were arranged to establish a strong visual hierarchy, enhancing the clarity and impact of the proposal's core message.

Ultimately, the design reflects the vision of KMD Architects, positioning the SF Ferry Terminal project as both a civic landmark and a contemporary architectural achievement. It communicates the firm’s commitment to sustainability, innovation, and transformative urban design, ensuring the proposal stands out while maintaining a refined aesthetic that speaks to the significance of the project.
